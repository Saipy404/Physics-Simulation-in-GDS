Instructions:
W/Arrow Up - Move up
A/Arrow Left - Move left
D/Arrow Right - Move right

Hold or Press LMB (Left Mouse Button) Anywhere to Move Force Field.

Dynamic Body - Orange Circle
Force Field - Small Blue Circle
